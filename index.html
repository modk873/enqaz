About Enqaz SOS App

Enqaz SOS is a simple and smart safety tool designed to help people during emergencies. We connect users with the closest available help, whether it's volunteers, police, or medical services.

  • Quick SOS: Send emergency alerts with your location instantly.
  • Smart Location: Find nearby volunteers and emergency services.
  • Community Help: Connect with local volunteers for immediate assistance.
  • Direct Contact: Call or message volunteers directly for quick help.
